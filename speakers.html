Become a Speaker at Conference Bitcoin Afrique 2025

Join a movement driving financial inclusion and economic development in French Speaking Africa.

Who We’re Looking For

We are seeking experienced and inspiring speakers with expertise in the following areas:

  • Bitcoin and Lightning Network: Innovations, use cases, and future potential.
  • Financial Inclusion: Leveraging Bitcoin and stablecoins for unbanked populations.
  • Fintech and Economic Growth: How fintech solutions drive development in Africa.
  • Open-Source Technology: The role of open-source tools in empowering communities.
  • Policy and Regulation: Insights into shaping favorable Bitcoin and blockchain policies.

What We Expect

  • Proven expertise and knowledge in your field.
  • A passion for educating and inspiring others.
  • Practical solutions and strategies for real-world challenges.

Why Speak at Our Event?

  • Showcase Your Expertise: Gain visibility across French Speaking Africa.
  • Expand Your Network: Connect with thought leaders, innovators, and policymakers.
  • Inspire Change: Help shape the narrative of Bitcoin as a tool for financial inclusion.
