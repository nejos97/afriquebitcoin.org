Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about Conference Bitcoin Afrique

What is Conference Bitcoin Afrique?
Conference Bitcoin Afrique is the premier conference for Bitcoin enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and developers in French Speaking Africa, aiming to promote financial inclusion and innovation.
How can I attend the event?
Tickets can be purchased through our official website. Early bird discounts are available for a limited time.
Are there networking opportunities?
Yes, the event includes dedicated networking sessions to connect with industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts.
Who can attend the conference?
The conference is open to anyone interested in Bitcoin, including professionals, students, and the general public.
What is included in the ticket price?
The ticket covers access to all sessions, workshops, and networking events. Refreshments and conference materials are also included.
Are there sponsorship opportunities?
Yes, we offer a range of sponsorship packages. Please visit our Sponsors section or contact us for more information.
How can I become a speaker?
Speaker applications are open on our website. Submit your proposal along with your profile for consideration.
What languages will be used during the conference?
The primary language of the conference is French, with some sessions available in English.
